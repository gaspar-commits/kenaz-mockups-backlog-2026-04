The principal

A London-trained dentist who made Jávea home.

Karen qualified from The Royal London Hospital College in 1988. After six years practising in London and Dorset she moved to Jávea in 1994 — and never looked back. She runs the clinic with the same principle she began with: take time, explain clearly, treat conservatively.

“Most of my patients arrived frightened of dentists. They stay with us because we listen first, and treat second.”

In addition to general dentistry, Karen holds advanced qualifications in orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry and facial rejuvenation. She works alongside Dr. Jorge Santillán (implants & prosthodontics) and a bilingual team who have been together for over a decade.
