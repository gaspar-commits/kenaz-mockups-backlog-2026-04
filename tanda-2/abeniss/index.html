02 · Days at Abeniss

Breakfast under the palm.
Then nothing to do.

A slow place. Bread from the village, olive oil from the fields around, fruit from the market in Benissa. The pool opens at eight. The rest of the day is yours — a hike to Bèrnia, a swim in the coves, or a long lunch nowhere.

  • 01
    A locally-sourced breakfast
    Served 8:30 to 10:30 on the terrace, or in your room. Included for all guests.
  • 02
    Pool, garden, and Balinese beds
    A 14-metre saltwater pool, palm trees, and quiet corners all day.
  • 03
    Routes we know by heart
    Peñón de Ifach, Cala Baladrar, wineries of Xaló — we make the map ourselves.

04 · The setting

Ten minutes from the sea.
A world away from it.

Abeniss sits in Partida Orxelles, on the rural slope of Benissa. From the terrace you see the Peñón de Ifach rising from the Mediterranean — the landmark of the whole Costa Blanca.
