The Denistars approach

A small team of chefs,
picked by hand.
No franchise, no filler.

We bring the restaurant to your villa — without the noise, the queue, or the compromise. One chef, one kitchen, one evening built around you.

Denistars was founded in Dénia in 2020 by chef Tudorel Caragata, with a deliberately small roster of chefs chosen for their personal style, not volume. Every service is booked direct — no agencies, no middlemen — so the chef who answers your first email is the chef who cooks in your kitchen.

Our clients are villa owners in Moraira and Jávea, destination couples celebrating along the Costa Blanca, and families spending summers between the Balearics and the Alicante coast. The menu shifts to fit the occasion; the standard does not.

— Chef Tudorel

Four ways
we cook for you.

From a single evening at home to a week on site with a full team, every engagement starts with the same enquiry and is shaped around the guests, the space, and the occasion.

I.

Private dinner at the villa

A chef arrives in the afternoon, cooks a tasting menu in your kitchen, serves at your table, and leaves the space cleaner than they found it. The most requested service for anniversaries, milestone birthdays, and arrival nights.

From 2 to 20 guests →
II.

Wedding & event catering

Full wedding service at your chosen venue: menu design, tasting, staffing, bar, setup, service, breakdown. We work with villa weddings and cliff-side ceremonies across the Costa Blanca and the Balearics.

From 20 to 120 guests →
III.

Long-term chef residency

A chef in residence for a week, a month, or the season. Breakfast, lunch, dinner — or whichever meals you keep. Shopping, prep, cooking, and a spotless kitchen are all part of the service.

One week minimum →
IV.

Home cooking classes

Paella, arroces, sushi and Mediterranean classics taught in your own kitchen, by a chef who cooks them for a living. A relaxed afternoon for families, villa guests, or private groups of friends.

Up to 10 participants →

From enquiry
to plated, in four steps.

i
Enquiry
Tell us the date, the villa, the number of guests, and the occasion. We reply within one working day.
ii
Consultation
A short call with the chef who will cook. Allergies, preferences, budget, style — all agreed before a menu is drafted.
iii
Bespoke menu
A written tasting menu, paired with wines from the region, sent for your approval. Unlimited adjustments until it's right.
iv
The evening
The chef arrives, cooks, serves, and leaves the kitchen spotless. You stay at the table with your guests.
