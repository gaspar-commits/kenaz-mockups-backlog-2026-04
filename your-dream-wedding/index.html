About

One planner. One couple at a time.

I’m Marcella Davis. I started planning weddings on the Costa Blanca eight years ago, after moving here from the UK with my own family. Everything that happens on your day passes through me first — the suppliers, the table, the timings, the quiet conversations in the weeks before. No account managers, no hand-offs.

The diary is capped at ten weddings a year so each one keeps the attention it deserves. We work in English throughout and handle every paper trail the Spanish registry needs, so you can arrive, rest, and marry.

  • 01 One point of contact. Marcella, from first note to last dance.
  • 02 Paperwork, handled. Civil and symbolic ceremonies, bilingual documents, registry liaison.
  • 03 A short supplier list. Photographers, florists and chefs we have worked with for years.
  • 04 Designed restraint. Every detail earns its place. No upsells, no filler.
