The craft

An artistic approach to every arrangement.

We take an artistic approach to making arrangements. Nothing comes out of a box, nothing is repeated. Each piece is composed the morning it leaves the shop, around what arrived fresh that day.

Fresh flowers are delivered from Spain and The Netherlands every day. That is the whole premise: real, living stock — never the same twice.

Weddings

Flowers for the day that only happens once.

Weddings are the part of the shop we look after most carefully. Bridal and bridesmaid bouquets, ceremony pieces, table centres and venue florals — each one composed around the bride, the venue and the flowers in season.

We work by personal consultation. You tell us the date, the place, the feeling you want, and we build the proposal from there.
