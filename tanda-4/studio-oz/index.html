01 · What we practise

Seven disciplines, one studio.

Yoga, Pilates, Barre and dance-based fitness under one roof. Classes are taught in English and welcome every body.

01 — Breath & flow

Yoga

Slow vinyasa and grounded flows to move with breath. Open to students new to the mat as well as long-time practitioners.

02 — Heated room

Hot Yoga

Practice in a warmer room to loosen muscles, deepen stretches and sweat out the week. Water and a mat are all you need.

03 — Ballet-inspired

Barre

Small, precise movements at the ballet barre to build long, toned muscles and posture without impact on the joints.

04 — Core & control

Pilates

Mat-based Pilates focused on the core, alignment and controlled breath. A practice for anyone rebuilding after injury.

05 — Build & tone

Strength

Functional strength work with bodyweight and small equipment. Designed to complement yoga and pilates, not replace them.

06 — Mobility

Stretch

A full session dedicated to mobility, releasing tight hips, shoulders and backs. Slow, restorative, deeply needed.

07 — Dance it out

Zumba

Latin rhythms and choreography for a cardio session that doesn't feel like one. No experience needed, just come to move.

03 · Who teaches here

Meet Sally & the team.

Studio OZ is led by Sally and supported by visiting teachers who bring their own lineage to the space.

Taught by people who have practised elsewhere.

"Our teachers come from all over the world."

Sally founded Studio OZ to bring a boutique yoga and movement culture to L'Alfàs del Pi. The studio keeps class sizes small so instructors can actually see you, adjust you, and remember you the next time you come in.

Alongside Sally, a rotating circle of guest teachers joins the studio each season — each one bringing a different voice to the same consistent space.
